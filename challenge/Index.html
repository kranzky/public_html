The Learning Machine Challenge

Can your approach break new ground in artificial intelligence?

We challenge you to create a program that can win at a game without knowing either the game's rules or the identity of its opponent. $2,000 will be awarded to the creator of the winning program. The deadline for entries is October 30, 2001.
