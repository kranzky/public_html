Abstract

This page contains details about my research. It's still heavily under construction at this stage, but that doesn't mean it's not worth having a look at. I haven't kept any of my research or experimental results hidden, I intend to put everything up here. Consult the contents to read about what each section contains. Each section has a short-cut next to the heading, so you don't need to navigate back through the table of contents each time.
