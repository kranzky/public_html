Information

This web site is a resurrected version of some PHP2 server-side scripts and a mSQL database back-end which I deployed in mid-1998 as part of my research. It has been updated to use PHP4 and a MySQL database backend. It is intended to be a proof-of-concept of the Ai Online Lab.

Frequently Asked Questions

To learn more about the experiments, and to avoid some of the mistakes which other people have been making, please consult the FAQ.

Rules

The rules and regulations are simple, but they are required to make sure that the experimental results are valid. The main incentive for following the rules is the chance to win money! Here are the rules you must follow:
  1. You must be a competent English speaker.
  2. You must only register once.
  3. Don't give anyone your password.
  4. Don't discuss the experiments with anyone who may also be participating.
  5. Don't enter false or misleading information.
  6. Follow the rules for each experiment exactly.
  7. Don't abuse the system in any way.
  8. You mustn't have a personal agenda!

Register

In order to become an experimental subject (AKA guinea pig) in these experiments, you need to register. Here are the reasons why:
  • I need to have some rough demographic information about the participants.
  • I need a way of ensuring that you're not abusing the system.
To register, please fill out the registration form. Registration takes about five minutes. You will be sent an email containing a password. Once you've received this confirmation email, enter your email address and password in the form below, and click the "log on" button.
