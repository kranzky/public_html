General Information

Help!

If you need help, and this FAQ doesn't answer your question, please email hutch@tmbg.org and I will do my best to help you.

What currency is the $100 in?

It is in Australian dollars. The exchange rate is fairly poor at the time of writing, but it should still represent a sizeable sum of money not matter where you're from. To find out how much money it represents in your currency, visit the Universal Currency Converter™.

Can I use the same email and password from home and work?

You sure can! The email address you entered is equivalent to your user name, so you can log in from anywhere... even someone elses account! Just enter your email address and your password.

How are YOU getting feedback?

I have developed a lot of online tools to analyse the experimental results, so I can quickly see how things are going. When you complete the experiment and opt to retire from it, you will be given access to a cut-down version of these tools.

How will the winner be chosen?

After six months have expired, I'll officially stop the experiment. Once this occurs, I will make a list of all people who have completed all of the experiments, and who have followed all of the rules. A user will be selected at random from this list, and declared the winner!

Registration

Where's my password?

Your password will be emailed to the email address you specified when registering. It is possible that there will be some delay sending this information to you. If you haven't received your password within 24 hours, please email hutch@tmbg.org and I will do my best to help you.

Why do I have to be aged 18 or over?

You don't have to anymore. I got many complaints, and thought it a bit unfair. So, from now on, there's no age restriction.

Why do I get an error when using Lynx?

We're not talking about the male cologne here, either. Some versions of the Lynx web browser seem to be incompatible with my experiment pages. I don't know what the problem is, so I can't fix it. I can only suggest that you try a different version of Lynx, or go over to the Dark Side and use another web browser. Sorry!

The Story Experiment

Why didn't my word appear in the story?

To add a word to one of the stories, you must type the word into the text box, and then PRESS ENTER. You will then be asked to confirm that you wish to add the word, and you must click the button to do this, otherwise your word won't be added.
