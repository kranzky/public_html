A HYBRID APPROACH



  • A greedy parser is used to read chunks from the input stream, with respect to the adaptive dictionary.
  • The encoder uses a chunk-level PPMC model to encode the chunk for transmission.
  • The chunker uses a symbol-level PPMC model to discover new chunks in the data.
  • The parser provides a crude form of Chunk Evaluation, as chunks are only added to the model when they are parsed.
