Introduction

This is my 1998 diary. The start of the year was spent holidaying down south (in Western Australia), and attending the Loebner Competition and a conference on Natural Language Learning. Upon my return I vowed to maintain a daily diary (well, at least on the week-days), and that is exactly what you'll find below!
