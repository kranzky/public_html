Introduction

In this section I give pointers to places around the Web where people are conducting research which is somehow related to my own.

Gerry Wolff

Gerry Wolff is in the School of Electronic Engineering and Computer Systems at the University of Bangor in Wales. His current work is Cognition as Compression, which is close to my philosophical stance. In the past, he has worked on language learning and text segmentation.

David MacKay

David MacKay is in the Cavendish Laboratory at Cambridge University in England. He is a diehard Bayesian, and has done work on improving statistical language models using Bayesian techniques.
