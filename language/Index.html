The Artificial Intelligence Language Laboratory

In this web site I intend to perform some interactive online experiments. The idea is that any member of the public can come in and look at the progress of some experiments, registered users can take part, Ai staff will see even more than that and administrators will be able to calculate results and so on. For the time being I will list some of the experiments.

  • An online version of HeX, the conversation simulator written by me which won the Loebner contest in 1996.
  • An online version of MegaHAL.
  • Babelfish translation experiments.
  • Collective mind story writing experiment.
  • Sentence smoothing experiment.
  • Prediction experiment.
  • Chunking experiment.
  • Infomagnetism sentence generation.
