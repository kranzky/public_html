Introduction

This is the companion Web site to Jason Hutchens' PhD thesis. On this Web site you can find the corpora mentioned in the thesis, most of the technical reports, theses, conference papers and technical presentations cited in chapter 8 of the thesis, and links to other Web sites of interest.
