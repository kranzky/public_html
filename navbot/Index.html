Jason Hutchens: navbot

NavBot is the initial mock-up implementation of a web-based version of the envisaged Ai natural language interface. At the moment you press F10 to pop-up the interface. You can then type the following commands:

  • close, bye, quit or exit to close the interface;
  • back to move back one page;
  • forward to move forward one page;
  • home to move to your home page;
  • navbot to return to this web page;
  • print to send the current page to the printer;
  • stop, interrupt or halt to stop the current transfer;
  • search xxx or find xxx to perform a search on google for xxx;
  • go xxx, url xxx or surf xxx to go to the URL xxx; and
  • email xxx or mail xxx to send an email to the address xxx.
